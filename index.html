Frequently Asked Questions

I have poor credit. Will I still be approved?

Yes. We can still get the funds for your business; however, the program is slightly modified. We have a starter package that you can join. Once your funding specialist understands you, and your business, your credit score becomes irrelevant.

What can I use the working capital for?

You can use your merchant cash advance towards any activity which will assist in the growth of your business or the maximization of profit. This can include expansions, acquiring new or additional inventory, purchasing new technologies or equipment, etc.

When will I receive the funds?

You could have your money in your business bank account by the end of the business day! Our team works efficiently as possible to ensure that you receive your desired advance as timely as possible. Call, Email or apply online now to be in touch with one of our funding managers!

How much money would I be eligible for?

You can receive an advance of as little as $10,000 to a maximum of $250,000 depending on different factors.
